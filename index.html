Why 30’ x 100’?

The cost of crop management and environmental management can start to increase at much higher rates when you move above a 30’ x 100’ space.
Permit Ready Greenhouses are optimized for maximum airflow, reduced micro-climates, and economical utility bills.
All Permit Ready Greenhouses for California are specially designed to cold snaps and humidity swings. Our 120mph rated structures are built for category 3 winds, and include light deprivation systems.
